Wildfire!

2020-04-27
non-tech

Fire all around you
Don’t go into fire
The firefighter comes. It is true
It is a wildfire!
The police comes and says “Stay inside!”
We are sad. We cried and cried
It is a wildfire!

- Maya Haridas
